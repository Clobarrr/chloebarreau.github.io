EAST VS WEST

×

SPEED TESTS WITH PYTHON

Learned the foundations of sending data over the Internet with HTTPs and APIs. Used Python because it was a new langauge, could be picked up quickly, and would be opimal for playing with data.

PURPOSE

Websites in China are often not optimized for the West and vice versa. The speed tests demonstrate the discrepancy. The startup I was working for, Notey, is creating a product that automatically converts Western resources to the Chinese equivalent (e.g. Google Maps to Baidu Maps, YouTube to YouKu etc)

CHALLENGES

Learned to dig into not just the program I was creating, but also the other resources I was leveraging. For a while I couldn't understand why I was reading incomplete JSON data and spent ages looking at more complicated solutions, such as polling, without extensively testing to understand the original problem. When I finally reverted, I realised I just needed to give the API more time (longer delay). This project taught me a couple of things 1) Yes, I was only two days into Python, but I should trust my instincts rather than rush to overcomplicate things. 2) Python is very easy to read, but lower level languages are more explicit and I was midly nostalgic when wanting to pass by reference.
